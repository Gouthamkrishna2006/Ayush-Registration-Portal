Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation and entrepreneurship in the field of traditional medicine and wellness by supporting startups aligned with the Ministry of Ayush's vision. Initiated by the Government of India, this project is dedicated to nurturing startups that harness the rich heritage of Ayurveda, Yoga, Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, Sowa-Rigpa, and Homeopathy (AYUSH). We aim to: Promote Traditional Medicine: Encourage the development and commercialization of AYUSH-based products and services that are rooted in India's ancient systems of health and wellness. Support Startup Growth: Provide mentorship, funding, and regulatory assistance to startups that are contributing to the AYUSH sector, enabling them to scale and innovate in a rapidly evolving marketplace. Enhance Public Health: Facilitate the integration of AYUSH practices into mainstream healthcare to enhance public health outcomes, focusing on preventive care, holistic treatment, and wellness. Global Outreach: Position India as a global leader in traditional medicine by promoting AYUSH startups that cater to international markets, showcasing the potential of these age-old practices in improving global health and well-being. By empowering startups, we are committed to driving economic growth, creating jobs, and building a healthier future for all.
